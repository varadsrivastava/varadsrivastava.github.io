Applied Brain and Cognitive Science Lab

Guten Tag, Researcher!

ABCS (Applied Brain and Cognitive Science) Lab is a virtual research space currently managed by Varad Srivastava. Varad is a student member of IEEE Brain Initiative and also has researcher access to the Human Brain Project (Co-funded by Europian Union). We focus on research in brain-computer interaction, biologically plausible deep learning and using understanding of the brain to make people lives better.

If you'd would like to collaborate, get in touch now through a mail at varadsrivastava@ieee.org !
